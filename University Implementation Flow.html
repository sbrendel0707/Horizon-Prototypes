Step 2 — Which integration methods will you use?
Select all that apply. Each method determines how data flows between your ERP and Nelnet. Review the descriptions below before selecting.
ℹ️
Most institutions use SSO + RTPN + RTAA as their core integration set. RTAN is commonly added when payment plan agreement tracking is needed. File Processing is used when real-time methods are not available or as a fallback.
SSO — Pass-Through Authentication
Recommended
Single sign-on: students authenticate once in your ERP and are passed seamlessly into Nelnet without logging in again.
How it works

Your ERP generates a time-sensitive authenticated token that is passed to Nelnet when a student navigates to the payment portal from your student self-service portal. The student is automatically logged in to Nelnet with their correct student account loaded — no separate username or password required.

Best for

All institutions. SSO significantly reduces login friction for students and prevents them from needing to manage a separate Nelnet credential. Strongly recommended for all implementations.

PeopleSoft Banner Workday Colleague
IT requirement: Your ERP or portal must be configured to pass an authentication hash/token to Nelnet's endpoint. Your Nelnet integration specialist will provide the endpoint URL and token format documentation.
RTPN — Real-Time Payment Notification
Recommended
Nelnet sends an instant notification to your ERP every time a payment is made, so your student account records update in real time.
How it works

When a payment is confirmed in Nelnet, a real-time HTTP POST notification is sent to a URL endpoint on your ERP. Your ERP processes the notification and posts the payment to the student's account immediately — no overnight batch processing required. The notification includes the payment amount, date, student ID, and transaction reference.

Best for

Institutions that want payments reflected on student accounts the same day they are made. Eliminates end-of-day reconciliation batches and allows students to see an updated balance immediately after paying.

PeopleSoft Banner Workday Colleague
IT requirement: Your ERP must expose a URL endpoint that accepts Nelnet's RTPN payload (XML or JSON). Your institution's IT team configures this endpoint; Nelnet provides the payload specification and will test connectivity before go-live.
RTAA — Real-Time Account Activity
Recommended
Nelnet queries your ERP in real time to pull a student's current charges and account balance whenever they log in to the payment portal.
How it works

Each time a student opens the Nelnet payment portal, Nelnet sends a real-time request to your ERP to retrieve the student's current account balance and itemized charges. This ensures the student always sees their most up-to-date balance — including new charges added after their last login — without requiring a manual sync or batch load.

Best for

Institutions where charges change frequently during the semester (add/drop, housing adjustments, late fees). Without RTAA, the student balance in Nelnet is only as fresh as your last data load.

PeopleSoft Banner Workday
IT requirement: Your ERP must expose a web service or API that accepts a student ID and returns charge/balance data in Nelnet's expected format. Nelnet provides the schema and will work with your IT team on the connection.
RTAN — Real-Time Agreement Notification
Optional
Nelnet notifies your ERP in real time when a student enrolls in or modifies a payment plan agreement, keeping plan records in sync.
How it works

When a student creates, modifies, or cancels a payment plan agreement in Nelnet, a real-time notification is sent to an endpoint in your ERP. This allows your student accounts team to see active payment plan enrollments directly in your ERP without logging into Nelnet — useful for advising, enrollment holds, and financial reporting.

Best for

Institutions that use payment plan enrollment status as a factor in enrollment holds or where the bursar team tracks plans inside the ERP. Also important for institutions that need plan data visible in external reporting tools connected to the ERP.

PeopleSoft Banner Workday
IT requirement: Same endpoint infrastructure as RTPN. If your institution is already implementing RTPN, adding RTAN is relatively low additional effort.
File Processing — EOD & Balance Compare
Optional
Scheduled batch file transfers between your ERP and Nelnet. Used for end-of-day reconciliation or as a fallback when real-time methods are not available.
How it works

Your ERP generates flat files (typically CSV or fixed-width) on a scheduled basis — usually end of day — and delivers them to a Nelnet SFTP location. Nelnet processes these files to load updated student balances, post payments, or reconcile differences between systems. A Balance Compare file can flag discrepancies between Nelnet and ERP balances so your team can investigate.

Best for

Institutions whose ERP does not support real-time web service integrations, or as a supplement to real-time methods for overnight reconciliation. Also used by institutions migrating from an older Nelnet setup that was file-based.
